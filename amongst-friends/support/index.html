common questions

FAQ

  • Do I need to use my real name?
    No. Your display name can be anything you like, it doesn't need to reflect your real identity in any way.
  • What personal data does the app store?
    Only your email address (used to sign in), your chosen display name, and the reviews or ratings you create. Nothing is linked to real-world identity beyond what you choose to share.
  • How do I delete my account?
    There is a "Delete Account & Data" button on the Profile page. This will delete all data.
  • I found a bug — what should I do?
    Email us with a description of the issue and, if possible, what steps led to it. Screenshots are always helpful.

legal

Privacy Policy

Amongst Friends collects only what's necessary to run the app: your email address for authentication, a display name of your choosing, and any reviews or ratings you submit. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising purposes.
